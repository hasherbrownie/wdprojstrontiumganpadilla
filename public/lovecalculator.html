Love Calculator

Calculate your love compatibility!

Throughout the story of *Miraculous Ladybug*, love is a central theme. A love square forms between Marinette, Adrien, and their superhero personas, creating a dynamic that is both a headache for the characters and a source of frustration for the fandom. As viewers grew attached to these characters, their tangled relationships became a point of emotional investment.

The love calculator ties into these themes, offering a fun way to calculate the compatibility between you and your partner based on criteria seen in the show. While it echoes the show's focus on relationships and teamwork, real-life relationships are far more complex and unpredictable. Success in relationships often depends on factors like emotional intelligence, personal competence, and your current life situation. The dynamic between Marinette and Adrien is heartwarming at times but, more often than not, infuriating. While we wish no one has to experience the stress these fictional characters face in finding their partners, it’s important to remember that basing real-life expectations on fiction is both disingenuous and unrealistic. Don’t put yourselves through unnecessary headaches searching for your match. Many real-life examples show that love doesn’t have to be a gamble. The love calculator simply allows fans to engage with that intrigue, experiencing the 'gamble' of love in a lighthearted way.

Before you input names, please answer these questions by checking the boxes (otherwise we're gonna assume all your answers to them are no)

1. Do you have secrets that could affect the relationship?

Hiding important parts of your life, like Marinette and Adrien, can create distance and misunderstanding in your relationships.
2. Are you impulsive?

Impulsive actions can lead to misunderstandings and complications, as seen with Marinette and Adrien. Taking time to reflect before acting can prevent unnecessary drama.
3. Are you unwilling to share your feelings and inner thoughts with them?

Like many characters, struggling to open up emotionally can hinder deeper connections. It is key to building trust and intimacy.
4. Would you harm others if you felt it meant better odds?

Throughout the show, Marinette does exactly that a lot, which isn't good, obviously.
