Introduction

Marinette Dupain-Cheng seems like an ordinary middle schooler, balancing her passion for fashion with helping her parents run the most beloved bakery in Paris. Adrien Agreste, on the other hand, is a famous model trying to navigate the pressures of his fatherâ€™s high expectations while coping with the pain of losing his mother. Despite their vastly different lives, they cross paths at school, where they begin to discover that they share more in common than they think.

Their lives take a dramatic turn when they are chosen to become Ladybug and Cat Noir, superheroes tasked with protecting Paris from powerful villains.
